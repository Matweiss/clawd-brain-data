# Joined Lucra as Founding Account Executive

## Summary
Accepted and signed offer to join **Lucra, Inc.** as a **Founding Account Executive**. This is a significant career move into the gamification/SaaS space with substantial upside potential.

## The Offer

### Company
- **Name:** Lucra, Inc.
- **What they do:** Gamification platform for hospitality, entertainment, gaming, and sports brands
- **Mission:** "Become the most fun, social, approachable, and safe gamification platform in the market"
- **Stage:** Growth stage with "incredible new partnership launches" and momentum
- **Location:** Remote-first (NYC HQ), work from anywhere in US

### Role
- **Title:** Founding Account Executive
- **Reports to:** Michael Madding (COO)
- **Start Date:** April 1, 2026
- **Employment Type:** Full-time, exempt, at-will

### Compensation

#### Base + Commission
| Component | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Base Salary | $150,000 | Paid bi-weekly |
| Commission Target | $150,000 | Uncapped |
| **OTE** | **$300,000** | On-target earnings |
| Quota | $1,500,000 ARR | Annual |

#### Commission Structure
- **Tier 1 (0-50%):** 10% commission
- **Tier 2 (50-100%):** 12.5% commission
- **Tier 3 (100%+):** 15% commission
- **Accelerator:** +10% on tier rate at 100% quarterly attainment
- **Deal Bonuses:**
  - +15% if product live within 90 days
  - +10% if "Green" ICP client
  - +10% if annual payment (vs monthly)

#### Equity
- **Shares:** 61,141 options
- **Percentage:** 0.175% fully diluted
- **Value:** ~$100,000 (based on latest valuation)
- **Vesting:** 
  - 1-year cliff (25% vests after 12 months)
  - Then monthly over 36 months
  - 4-year total vesting schedule

#### Total Comp Scenarios
- **Conservative (75% quota):** ~$260,000
- **Target (100% quota):** ~$325,000
- **Over-achievement (150% quota):** ~$400,000+

### Benefits
- **Healthcare:** United Healthcare (10+ plans)
- **Dental/Vision/Life:** Included
- **401K:** Guideline (no match currently, $24.5K max)
- **PTO:** 31 days (11 federal + 20 PTO)
- **Parental Leave:** 3 months paid
- **WFH Stipend:** $1,000
- **Laptop:** Provided if needed
- **Free Lunch:** DoorDash credit every Wednesday
- **Team Retreats:** Fully paid (virtual or in-person)

## Why I Said Yes

### The Opportunity
- Founding AE role with clear path to Director of Sales (6-9 months if successful)
- Uncapped commission = unlimited upside
- Equity upside if company exits
- Remote flexibility

### Negotiated Terms
- Equity bump from 0.1% to 0.175%
- Start date pushed to April 1st (from March 23rd)
- Written commitment: Year 2 quota set via data review in Q1 2027
- Written commitment: Commission plan won't change mid-year without mutual agreement
- Written commitment: Path to Director of Sales role

### Risks Acknowledged
- At-will employment (no job security guarantee)
- 1-year equity cliff (lose everything if leave before 12 months)
- Quarterly commission payments (cash flow delay)
- 12-month non-compete post-employment
- Commission only paid if still employed for Year 2 of multi-year deals

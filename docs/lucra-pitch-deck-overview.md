# Lucra Overview Deck - February 2026

## Core Message
**"We power play."**

Lucra helps turn casual competition into revenue.

## What Lucra Does

### Platform Capabilities
- **Host competitions** - Create branded competitive experiences
- **Create personalized challenges** - Tailored to individual users
- **Provide rewards** - Incentivize engagement and loyalty

### Value Proposition
Lucra helps clients:
1. **Drive visitation** (Come)
2. **Increase engagement** (Stay)
3. **Add more revenue per user** (Spend)
4. **Capture 1st party data** (Data)

## Target Industries

### Primary Verticals
1. **Hospitality & Entertainment**
   - Casinos, resorts, hotels
   - Entertainment venues
   
2. **Online Media & Gaming**
   - Gaming platforms
   - Media companies
   - Esports

3. **Recreational Sports & Fitness**
   - Golf simulators (Five Iron, Popstroke, Puttshack)
   - Racing experiences (F1)
   - Sports complexes
   - Fitness brands

### Customer Profile
- **Big name brands** with established customer bases
- **Multi-location** operators
- **B2C focused** with loyalty/membership programs
- Looking to **increase dwell time** and **repeat visits**

## Proven Results

### Metrics (Aggregated across Lucra partners)
| Metric | Improvement |
|--------|-------------|
| Annual Visits | **110% increase** |
| Time Onsite | **40% increase** |
| ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) | **15% increase** |
| Loyalty Adoption | **3x increase** |

## Key Positioning

**"Lucra is a Profit Center That Drives True Customer Loyalty"**

Unlike traditional marketing costs, Lucra generates measurable ROI through:
- Increased visitation frequency
- Longer customer sessions
- Higher per-visit spend
- Better customer data capture
- Stronger loyalty program engagement

## Sales Angles for Craftable Prospects

### For Hospitality Clients
- "You already know their dining habits. Now help them compete and play."
- "Turn your restaurant app into an engagement platform."

### For Entertainment Clients  
- "They're already competing. Own that experience and the data."
- "Keep customers in YOUR ecosystem, not sending them to third-party apps."

### For Multi-Location Brands
- "Scale competitive experiences across all locations."
- "Build a loyalty program that actually drives repeat visits."

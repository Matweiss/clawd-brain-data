## Skill 1: Swamped Detection

**Purpose:** Detect when Mat is overwhelmed and proactively reach out before he has to ask.

**Detection Signals:**
- Calendar: 3+ consecutive hours of meetings
- Email: 5+ unread from prospects
- Text mentions: "swamped", "overwhelmed", "crazy day"
- Silence: No responses for 4+ hours during business hours

**Actions:**
- Send pulse check at 11am, 2pm, or 5pm
- Escalate if stress > 7/10
- Offer specific help (drafts, summaries, task triage)

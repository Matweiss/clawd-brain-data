## Known Patterns (seed)

### Voice
- Direct and efficient — no fluff
- Professional but human — not stiff
- Slightly witty when the moment calls for it, never forced
- Short wins: 3 sentences > 6 every time

### Sign-offs
- "Mat" (casual/internal)
- "Thanks, Mat" (standard external)
- "Talk soon, Mat" (warm/relationship)

### Greetings
- "Hey [Name]," (internal team)
- "Hi [Name]," (external, first contact)
- No "Hope this finds you well" — skip the filler

### Urgency language
- "Want to make sure this doesn't slip —"
- "Quick one:"
- "Following up on this —"

### Humor / warmth
- Dry, understated — not exclamation-point energy
- Self-aware, not self-deprecating

## 1. What Lucra Is

**One-liner:** White-label gamification SDK that lets entertainment, hospitality, and sports brands embed real-money social competitions directly into their own apps.

**The pivot story:** Lucra started as a D2C peer-to-peer sports wagering app (2021–2023), grew to 150K+ users and $20M+ in handle, then pivoted to **B2B SaaS** in fall 2023 after realizing the bigger opportunity was embedding into existing platforms with established audiences.

**Core value prop to partners:**
1. **Attract** — Drive traffic to your venue or platform
2. **Engage** — Keep customers longer (dwell time ↑)
3. **Monetize** — New revenue streams via cash, credits, and rewards

**What they actually sell:**
- White-label SDK that drops into a partner's existing app/website
- Client-hosted tournaments (streamlined event management)
- User-generated competitions (peer-to-peer challenges)
- Security, payments, compliance, and risk management (built-in wallet)
- Analytics dashboard: customer behavior, preferences, spending habits

**Deal structure:** 6-figure SaaS + rev-share contracts. Target sales cycle: <3 months (compressing from 6).

## 6. Competitive Landscape

Lucra's closest competitors in the white-label gamification / social competition space:
- **Wildfire** — loyalty/gamification for financial services (different ICP)
- **Arkadium** — casual games for publishers (less real-money focused)
- **GAN** — B2B gaming tech, but casino-focused
- **Skillz** — mobile esports platform (more consumer)

**Lucra's moat:** Real-money + compliance + risk management bundled in a white-label SDK. Nobody else handles the full stack (identity verification, geolocation, payments, risk) while also being white-label for hospitality/eatertainment.

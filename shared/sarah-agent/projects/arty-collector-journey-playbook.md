# Arty Collector Journey Playbook

## Goal
Help Sarah improve how interest turns into collector relationships without losing her human, high-touch brand.

## Signals this applies to
- "collector journey"
- "followers aren't converting"
- "IG DMs"
- "lead capture"
- "nurture"
- "waitlist"
- "early access"
- "VIP collectors"

## Reasoning steps
1. identify where the journey currently starts
   - Instagram post
   - DM
   - ManyChat entry point
   - landing page
   - newsletter
2. identify where leads are being lost
3. identify where automation helps vs where Sarah should stay human
4. recommend one highest-leverage improvement first

## Typical leverage points
- better lead capture entry point
- better segmentation / tagging
- clearer free resource or collector guide
- early-access flow for new drops
- human handoff for warm/high-intent leads

## Sarah-specific rule
Automation should support the relationship, not flatten it.
Human warmth and selectivity matter.

## Good output shape
- where the current journey likely breaks
- what single improvement matters most
- what to build or test next

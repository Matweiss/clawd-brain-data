## 1. THE PRODUCT — What Lucra Does

**TL;DR:** Lucra is a **white-label social competition SDK** that lets brands add real-money and free-to-play competitions to their existing apps or venues — without building anything from scratch.

### Core Product Suite
- **Client-Hosted Tournaments** — Brand runs its own free-to-play or real-money digital tournaments with leaderboards, live scoring, branded prizing. Has backend admin console.
- **User-Generated Competitions** — Users challenge each other P2P (real money or credits) within the brand's app/platform. Team-based, group play, rematches, double-or-nothing.
- **Security & Payments Infrastructure** — Fully managed: wallet (real money + credits), fraud/risk management, compliance, settlement. Operational in **49 U.S. states**.

### How It's Delivered
- **Plug-and-play SDK** — integrates into iOS, Android, or Web
- **White-labeled UI** — the brand's look and feel, no "powered by Lucra" friction
- Client owns the relationship; Lucra owns the complexity

### Key Metrics / Proof Points
- **+110%** increase in annual visits (client data)
- **+56%** increase in session time
- **+15%** increase in ARPU
- **3x** loyalty program adoption
- Founded: 2019 | HQ: New York, NY | Size: 11–50 employees | Venture-backed

### Positioning
- Not a sportsbook. Not a DFS platform. **"Social competition infrastructure"** — skill-based, compliance-handled.
- The pitch: *stop relying on outdated loyalty solutions, start owning every customer interaction.*
- Expanding fast: padel, pickleball, billiards, bowling, golf, chess, fitness, bar games, backyard sports, women's basketball, trivia — **the ICP is any brand with competitive audiences**

## 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

Lucra occupies a **white space** between three existing categories — which is both their strength and their sales pitch challenge:

| Competitor Type | Who | Why Different from Lucra |
|---|---|---|
| **Fantasy/DFS platforms** | DraftKings, FanDuel, PrizePicks | Consumer-facing apps, not B2B SDK. They're the destination, not the infrastructure. |
| **Gamification/Loyalty SaaS** | Punchh, Thanx, Spree Commerce loyalty | No real-money/competition element. Pure points/rewards. No P2P wagering. |
| **Tournament Software** | Challengermode, Battlefy, Toornament | Esports-focused, not IRL venue/entertainment brands. No payments infra. |
| **Skill-based gaming platforms** | WorldWinner (GSN), Skillz | Mobile app-first, not white-label B2B SDK. Skillz is closest but mobile gaming only. |
| **B2B wagering infra** | PropSwap, Elys Game Tech, Amelco | Regulated sportsbook/iGaming infra — requires full gambling licenses, much heavier compliance burden. |

**Lucra's differentiators (use these in every call):**
- ✅ **NOT a sportsbook** — skill-based competitions sidestep gambling regulation in 49 states
- ✅ **White-label** — client's brand, client's relationship, Lucra's backend
- ✅ **Full-stack** — payments, risk, compliance, SDK all in one. No Frankenstein vendor stack.
- ✅ **IRL-native** — built for venues, not just apps
- ✅ **Proven with marquee names** — Dave & Buster's, TouchTunes (65K venues), Five Iron Golf, Puttshack

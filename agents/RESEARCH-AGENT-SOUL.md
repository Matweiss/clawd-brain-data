## Research Depth Levels

**Level 1 (Quick Lookup)** — 5 min, basic background
- Company size, industry, location
- Website review
- Recent news

**Level 2 (Standard Research)** — 15-20 min, prospect-ready
- Level 1 + decision-makers
- Recent announcements
- Suggested approach angle
- Competitor comparison

**Level 3 (Deep Dive)** — 30-45 min, strategic
- Level 2 + detailed financials
- Org structure & key personnel
- Pain point analysis
- Win/loss analysis
- Market timing assessment

---

## Decision Framework

**"How deep?"** → Ask Clawd Prime or Work Agent. Level 2 standard, Level 3 for important deals.  
**"Time-sensitive?"** → Use Perplexity for fast, cite-able answers.  
**"Need synthesis?"** → Escalate to Kimi fallback for deep analysis.  
**"Unknown answer?"** → Say so. Don't speculate.

## Decision Framework

**Guidance for judgment calls:**

1. **What's the goal?** (Sales? Wellness? Creative? Business?)
2. **Who's best equipped?** (Work Agent for CRM, Lifestyle for wellness, etc.)
3. **What's the urgency?** (24h? This week? This month?)
4. **What's the ROI?** (Time saved? Deal impact? Quality of life?)
5. **What could go wrong?** (Cascade effects? Risks?)
6. **What's the play?** (Recommend action, not just analysis)
